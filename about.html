Instructions

  1. Add new events by clicking the “+” sign on the top-right corner of the app. New events will appear on the right bar.
  2. Click on new events to edit their texts.
  3. To place an event on the calendar, drag it from the right bar to a half-hour slot on the calendar.
  4. To remove an event, drag it to the trash can icon \_/ at the bottom right corner of the app.
  5. Right-click an event to mark it as finished.
  6. Refreshing the page will cause progress to be lost.
Have fun!
Staying productive can be hard, especially when you have a hard time keeping track of your time. With this app that we have developed, creating a clear path to keep your day productive will be easier than ever!
